My

Workflow

When it comes to designing websites, all my projects start with a Figma model, where we discuss and try to find an agreement on the design and overlay of the project we are working together on. The choice of colors, fonts, overlay, elements on the sites, and overall design structure are capital in building the best web experience the internet has to offer.

I usually work with vanilla HTML, CSS and Javascript for small Front-End project as it allows me to work more freely on the architecture of the project. For bigger Front-End projects, I like to work with React JS alongside Tailwind CSS.

My experience with Back-End technologies is limited but I have worked with NodeJS and PHP in the past, and I am always looking to improve my knowledge of Back-End applications.
